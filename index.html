About Us

Trodly is marketplace for travellers to discover and book amazing activites for their travel destinations. For businesses, Trodly is a partner platform to list and make their activites available for booking online or on mobile while on the go.

Be it a half day city tour or a multi-day scuba diving certification course or a 2 week long expedition to a Himalayan peak, Trodly makes a variety of travel experiences available to travellers from around the globe. And with a dedicated team to provide for the best customer experience, Trodly is the go-to platform to discover and book your travel experiences.

Your travel does not end at booking flights and hotels; it starts there.
